
# AI Impact on Job Sector Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes how Artificial Intelligence (AI) impacts employee productivity, salaries, automation risk, job satisfaction, and workforce transformation across multiple industries using Python and Power BI.

The project includes data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and interactive dashboard creation to generate meaningful business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze the impact of AI on jobs and workforce transformation
- Identify industries with high AI adoption
- Detect jobs with high and low automation risk
- Understand salary and productivity changes after AI adoption
- Support better career and business decision-making

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
- **Source:** Kaggle – AI Impact on Job Sector Dataset
- **Records:** 2000 employee records
- **Features:** 17 columns
- **Domain:** Human Resource (HR)

### Key Features
- Employee ID
- Age
- Gender
- Education Level
- Industry
- Job Role
- AI Adoption Level
- Automation Risk
- Salary Before AI
- Salary After AI
- Job Satisfaction
- Productivity Change %

## 💡 Key Insights
- Medium and High AI adoption levels dominate the workforce.
- AI adoption improved employee productivity.
- Salaries increased moderately after AI implementation.
- Remote work employees showed slightly higher job satisfaction.
- Higher education levels resulted in better salary outcomes.
- Automation risk was higher in repetitive job roles.
- AI is transforming jobs more than replacing them.

---

## ✅ Conclusion
This project demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence is reshaping the workforce through productivity improvement, salary growth, automation, and job transformation. The analysis highlights the importance of upskilling, data-driven decision-making, and balancing AI with human involvement in modern industries.
